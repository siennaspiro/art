Sienna Spiro

“Born of earth, shaped by flame, and carried on breath.”

The name Sienna comes from terra di Siena — the clay-rich soil of Tuscany, where iron and manganese oxide stain the land.

Spiro evokes breath and motion — breath spiraling like smoke through time.
